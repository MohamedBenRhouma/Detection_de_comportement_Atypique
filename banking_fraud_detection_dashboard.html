Model Interpretation Guide
Score = -1 (Anomaly) Transaction is flagged as suspicious. Low anomaly score means the path to isolate this point was short — it's an outlier far from the data cluster.
Score = +1 (Normal) Transaction behaves like the majority. High score means many splits were needed to isolate it — it's buried deep in the dense region of normal behavior.
Contamination = 5% Tells the model to expect ~5% fraud. Tune this based on domain knowledge. Higher = more flagged transactions. Lower = stricter detection threshold.
